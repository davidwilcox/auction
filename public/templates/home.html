SVUUS Auction

Welcome to the SVUUS Auction Website! Thank you for participating. It should be a great party!

To get started on the site, click the “Register” button above to create an account. Have a photo of yourself and/or your family ready to upload. This is useful so that if you donate an item, others can see who you are and who donated it. Try to use a closeup photo of faces.

Second, buy tickets for you and your family members who are attending.

Note #1: There are activities for kids ages 5 and up—but there is nothing for kids age four and below, so we recommend you keep them with a babysitter that night.

Note #2: The credit card you use to buy your tickets will be used to pay your final invoice after the auction.

Third, donate items to auction. If you need suggestions, you can click below.

Donation Ideas

If you have not been to one of our auctions before, there’s a “Donation Ideas” button on the donation page that’ll get you started.

The Auction will start promptly at 6:00pm on Saturday, November 12th. There are no physical tickets. Just show up and we’ll give you your bid card.
